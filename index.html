Surrounded - Worldbuilding

Inspirations

Surrounded draws inspiration from Warhammer 40K, Black Myth: Wukong, Forever Winter, The Eight Hundred, Trench Crusade, and more.

Major Factions

The Golds: A coalition of oppressed civilizations fighting for sovereignty.

The Whites: A dominant power enforcing conformity through imperial ideology.

The Union for All Mankind: A former spacefaring civilization fighting for survival.
